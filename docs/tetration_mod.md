```cpp
template <typename T>
T tetration_mod(T x, T y, T M);
```

It returns $x \uparrow\uparrow y \pmod{M}$.

Note: In this function, $0^0$ is $1$.

## Constraints
- $x \geq 0$
- $y \geq 0$
- $M \geq 1$
