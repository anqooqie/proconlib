```cpp
template <typename C>
constexpr auto ssize(const C& c) -> std::common_type_t<std::ptrdiff_t, std::make_signed_t<decltype(c.size())>>;
```

It is a compatible alternative of `std::ssize` in C++20.

## Constraints
- `c.size()` is defined.

## Time Complexity
- Same as the time complexity of `c.size()`.
